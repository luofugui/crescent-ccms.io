## 青少年民乐团

北卡民乐团青少年民乐团面向热爱中国民族音乐的青少年成员开放。项目采用会费制，帮助学生在稳定的排练和演出安排中提升合奏能力、舞台经验和对中国音乐文化的理解。

The NCCME Youth Orchestra welcomes young musicians who are interested in Chinese traditional music. The program is membership-based and gives students a steady ensemble environment to grow their musicianship, performance confidence, and cultural connection.

### 适合成员

欢迎正在学习或已经具备一定基础的青少年民族器乐学生加入，包括二胡、琵琶、古筝、笛子、扬琴、中阮等中国民族乐器。具体分组和声部安排将根据报名情况和学生程度确定。

Young musicians studying Chinese instruments such as erhu, pipa, guzheng, dizi, yangqin, zhongruan, and related instruments are welcome. Ensemble placement and parts will be arranged based on enrollment and each student's level.
